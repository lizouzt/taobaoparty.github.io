Fees

  • Product fee
    The product fee is shown on the product page.
    If you can see 2 prices on the product page, TaobaoParty can help you buy it with discount price.

  • Domestic express fee
    The shipping fee from seller address to TaobaoParty warehouse address.
    Normal express companies take 3-5 days, the first 1kg need 10-15 Yuan, the additional 1kg need 5-10 Yuan.
    SF express and EMS express take 1-3 days, 20-30 Yuan/kg.
    Buy many items from same seller will save a lot on domestic express fee.
    A lot of sellers free domestic express fee now.

  • Commission
    TaobaoParty MEMBER commission = (products fee + domestic express fee) * 5%
    TaobaoParty VIP commission = (products fee + domestic express fee) * 3%
    TaobaoParty SVIP commission = (products fee + domestic express fee) * 2%
    TaobaoParty TRADER free commission!
    The commission is 50 Yuan per order, but there is no minimum order requirement.
    How to become TaobaoParty VIP, SVIP and TRADER?

  • International delivery fee
    The shipping fee from TaobaoParty warehouse address to your address.
    TaobaoParty international delivery fee list

  • Insurance fee
    Insurance fee is optional, it is 5% of total fee.
    In usual, the package can get delivered to your address safety.
    But if you do not buy insurance, if your package lost or damaged some products during the transit, we will not be responsible for it.
    Learn more about TaobaoParty insurance

  • PayPal fee
    If you choose PayPal make payment, PayPal will deduct fee (4.4%+0.3 USD) from the amount you paid.
    It mean Amount we received ≠ Amount you paid.

Amount We Received = Amount You Paid - (Amount You Paid * 4.4% + 0.3 USD)
For example, if you pay 1000 USD, we can only get 1000 - (1000 * 4.4% + 0.3) = 955.7 USD
So, according to the formula above:
Amount You Paid = (Amount We Reveived + 0.3 USD) / 95.6%
For example, if your order total cost 1000 USD, you need pay (1000 + 0.3) / 95.6% = 1046.34 USD
