How to search my favorite items on taobao?

Order Guide order guide 2018/01/15

Taobao.com shown in Chinese language, if you want to search something on taobao, you need translate search words from your language to Chinese.


  1. Open your browser, access https://translate.google.com.
  2. Choose your language in the left, then fill out the product name in the box. (Let us use language “English” and product name “sport shoes” for example.)
  3. Choose language “Chinese (simplified)” in the right, the below box will automatically display the corresponding product name in Chinese. Then copy the Chinese words.

  4. Access https://www.taobao.com.
  5. Paste the Chinese words into taobao search box.
  6. Click “搜索” button.

  7. Taobao will show the search results, you can see a lot of sport shoes below. TaobaoParty strongly suggest click “销量” option, then the best selling will show first.
  8. Choose your favorite sport shoes, click the picture.

  9. You can see the product page now, please copy the page link, you need paste it into TaobaoParty order form if you want to order it.
  10. The product price shown on this page, for example, this sport shoes price is “169 Yuan”.

of course, if you still can not search your favorite items, please contact our customer service, we will help you until you are satisfied.
