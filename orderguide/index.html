Order Steps

  • Step 1
    Search your favorite products on www.taobao.com or any other online stores, copy the products links.
    If you do not know how to search on taobao, please contact our customer service for help.

  • Step 2
    Please Download Order Form (Excel form) on our website, or contact our customer service, we will send a new order form to you.
    Then fill out the yellow cells in the order form.
    When you finished order form, please send it to our customer service by email xybbuy01@hotmail.com or skype.

  • Step 3
    Our customer service will calculate the first payment of your order within 24 hours.
    The first payment is total products fee + domestic express fee + commission.
    After we get your first payment, our purchaser will buy your order within 24 hours.
    And please attention your email or skype, if your items out of stock or any other problems, our customer service will tell you at the first time.

  • Step 4
    When all your products arrive our warehouse (3-7 days after purchase), our customer service will send items photo to you for check.
    We will also tell you the information of parcel weight and the international shipping fee.
    Then please make the final payment.

  • Step 5
    After we get your final payment, we will tell you the track number and send parcel to you within 24 hours.
