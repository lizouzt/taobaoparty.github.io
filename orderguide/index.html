Order Steps
Choose items on taobao Place order to TaobaoParty TaobaoParty purchase TaobaoParty delivery Get your parcel

  • Step 1
    Search your favorite products on www.taobao.com or any other online stores, save the products links.
    If you do not know how to search on taobao, please contact our customer service for help.
    How to search my favorite items on taobao?

  • Step 2
    Please Download Order Form (Excel form) on our website, or contact our customer service, we will send a new order form to you.
    Then fill out the yellow cells in order form.
    After finished order form, please send it to our customer service by email or skype.

  • Step 3
    Our customer service will calculate the first payment of your order within 24 hours.
    The first payment is total products fee + domestic express fee + commission.
    After we got your first payment, our purchaser will buy your order within 24 hours.
    And please attention your email or skype, if your items out of stock or any other problems, our customer service will tell you at the first time.

  • Step 4
    When all your products arrive our warehouse, our customer service will send items photo to you for check.
    You will also get the information of parcel weight and the international shipping fee.
    Then please make the final payment.

  • Step 5
    After we got your final payment, we will tell you the track number and send parcel to you within 24 hours.
